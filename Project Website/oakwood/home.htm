Quick facts

Team Frequency Tracker is a capstone based team assigned to the patient tracker project. They are composed of 4 skilled members as well as a team mentor.

 

General Information

This is the homepage for Frequency Trackers, the team consists of Kimberly Oyama, Chihiro Sasaki, Daren Rodhouse, and Blayne Kennedy. Also Dr. John Georgas is the mentor for the team and more infomation on both the team members as well as Dr. Georgas can be found in our Meet the Team section or Here.

About the Project

The project focuses on localization with a mobile Commerce app. Its the teams job to develop software to track consumers using the app Moneytrack from producers and provide them with infomation on another producer based on the data collected from past consumers. To get a better understanding of the project in more depth see our Project section or Here.
